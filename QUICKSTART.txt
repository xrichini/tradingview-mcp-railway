🆘 TROUBLESHOOTING

Q: My Railway URL doesn't work?
A: Wait 2 min. Check Railway dashboard → Deployments → Logs for errors.

Q: Claude still sees a timeout?
A: Make sure URL in Claude settings matches exactly (no trailing slashes).
   Test URL in browser: https://your-url.railway.app
   Should show MCP info.

Q: Why is Railway better than Render for this?
A: Render spins down after 15 min → Claude.ai timeout (30 sec wake-up).
   Railway never spins down → Claude.ai gets instant response.

Q: Is it really free?
A: Yes, $5 monthly credits = free for 3-6 months.
   After: $5/month if you keep it running (or switch to GitHub Actions).

Q: How do I see logs?
A: Railway Dashboard → Your service → Deployments → Logs
